Lemon Pound Cake Recipe


Photo by Karyna Panchenko on Unsplash

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅔ cup salted sweet cream butter, softened
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons lemon extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Cream sugar and butter together in a bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  3. Whisk flour and baking powder together in a separate bowl; gradually beat into creamed butter mixture until just combined.
  4. Beat eggs, 1 at a time, into butter-flour mixture until dough is thoroughly mixed, then stir in lemon extract.
  5. Drop small scoops of dough onto a baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies spring back when touched, 10 to 12 minutes.
